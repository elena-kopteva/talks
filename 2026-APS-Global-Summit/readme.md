**Title:**  Isotropic Coordinates for Generalized Schwarzschild-like Black Holes with Anisotropic Media  

**Venue/Date:** APS Global Summit, 2026 (online)  

**Abstract:** In this project, we develop isotropic representations of generalized Schwarzschild-like black hole spacetimes sourced by multiple noninteracting anisotropic fluids. The goal is to obtain conformally flat spatial slices that remain regular at the horizon and are useful for numerical relativity initial data, Hamiltonian and conformal formulations, and perturbative studies. These backgrounds help isolate environmental effects of non-vacuum matter distributions from intrinsic strong gravity signatures in scattering, lensing, and waveform modeling.
